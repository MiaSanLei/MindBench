Refugee Youth Empowerment Project- Deliverables

Marketing Plan

 Creating Project awareness among relevant Stakeholders

 Designing & Printing Brochures + Posters in Swedish along with translations in Dari, Arabic, Somali & English

 Launching a webpage explaining the project Details

 Meetings at 5 Refugee homes to provide first hand knowledge, sharing about the objectives, selection criteria & distributing of brochures

 Distribution of forms to gauge basic interest, previous education, language competencies

 Information meetings with the Management of the Refugee homes

Signup Process

 Collection & Selection of Prospective Participants on the basis of their age group (15 to 30 years), Availability & Intention to participate through the Project Implementation Phase, of not being in the Transit phase, of having Basic Literacy competency

 Inviting the Participants for Signup Process & having translators where needed

 Select 40 Participants

 Hiring Interpreters for these Languages: Dari, Somali & Arabic to the Swedish language

Information Sessions

 Orientation meetings where the relevant Organizations would give information regarding opportunities for business development, career opportunities & training sessions

 Giving information about Coaching Sessions

Coaching

 Advertise for voluntary certified coaches, or coaches from the Stakeholders

 Hire/Signup voluntary certified coaches

 Perform Initial 1 hour coaching sessions with aim to shape an idea for futher development

 Include hard Info sessions to groups of the participants on where help can be given at different associations. Also inform them about the coaching session structure, its aims such as to encourage participants to explore their own ideas, to formulate their own solutions & develop their own plans. The Coaches will assist them by providing advice, making connections & providing intelligence on Market & Industries

 1 week later: Perform 45 min. coaching session aimed at the Coachee developing concrete plan with timeliness, part of the plan being within the following two weeks.

 2 weeks later: Perform final 45 min. coaching session aimed at developing long term plan & further strategies.

Training Sessions and Information Meetings

 Meetings for those working at the youth homes to train on how to provide the needed support for the participants

Group Activities

 Inform Participants about the Group activities at Mollans Basement

 Allow scheduling & necessary resources that will help provide Mollans Basement with the needed requirements such as translators.

Evaluation of Participants

 Create a survey to evaluate Participants' well-being & motivation before the coaching sessions start & after the coaching session have ended

 Long Term Evaluation (2+years): Create a Survey that will evaluate where the participants are in their career development, (are the employed, not employed or working independently).
