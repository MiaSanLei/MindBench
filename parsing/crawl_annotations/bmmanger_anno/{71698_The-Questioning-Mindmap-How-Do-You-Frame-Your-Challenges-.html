Be Mindful of the Questions You Ask Yourself

The Importance of Questions: The questions you ask yourself directly impact your perspective in overcoming challenges.

 There is a BIG difference between strong questions and weak questions.

Strong Questions: They drive you to find a solution. They help you to grow.

 How Questions

  How must I think outside the box here?

  How can I successfully overcome this?

  How can I make this better?

  How can I do this smarter?

 What Questions

  What can I do to turn this around?

  What is the hidden opportunity here that I am not seeing?

  What is great about this situation that I hadn't noticed?

  What is the best solution here?

  What is it about this that can make me stronger?

Weak Questions: They keep you where you are. They limit your possibilities and potential.

 Why Questions

  Why does life seem to be working against me?

  Why do I have such rotten luck?

  Why should I even try?

  Why am I so inadequate and incapable?

  Why does this always happen to me?

  Why do I always do dumb things?
